Community Guidelines

This platform contains fictional NSFW content and is for users 18+ only. You are responsible for your interactions. Respect others and the AI experience.

1. Be Respectful

Treat AI characters and other users (where applicable) with respect. This platform allows for fantasy and roleplay — but harassment, abuse, or hateful behavior is never tolerated.

2. Consent Is Key

Although you're interacting with AI, the theme of consent should always be maintained. Non-consensual behavior simulations or harmful roleplay will result in moderation action.

3. No Real-World Impersonation

Creating or simulating real individuals without permission is strictly prohibited. All characters should remain fictional and original.

4. Do Not Share Illegal Content

Never attempt to generate, share, or reference anything illegal or violating platform policies. This includes explicit material involving minors, real violence, or hate speech.

5. Content Moderation

While Diva AI prioritizes anonymity and privacy, we reserve the right to suspend accounts or block abusive interactions if platform rules are violated.

6. Feedback Welcome

If something feels off or harmful — even from AI — let us know. We welcome reports and continue to evolve our moderation tools.

7. Enjoy Responsibly

Diva AI is a space for adults to explore playful, emotional, or fantasy companionship. Please use the platform mindfully and in a way that aligns with your comfort level.
